An Introduction to the Draft League Format

Essentially, it’s set up like fantasy football if you’ve ever played that. 
You each take turns picking Pokemon for your team until everyone has 8-13 depending on the league. Let's say you have 12.

Each week you battle one of the other teams using only Pokemon from your 12 that you “drafted” (picked). 
The other team can also only use mons from their 12 - which means you and your opponent both know what Pokemon you can bring and you have to counter team them.

Once a Pokemon is drafted, no other team can pick that Pokemon. 

Pokemon are usually divided into tiers based on how strong they usually are in this format. 
You have a limited amount of points and have to spend them wisely and pick the Pokemon you want before someone else can.

The whole thing is divided into seasons with each season lasting a few weeks + playoffs. 
The teams with the best records in their divisions fight in playoffs for the championship.

It's a completely different metagame than standard, and it gives opportunity to some very niche Pokemon to shine. 
Draft league is my favorite format and I highly advise all Pokemon fans to try it.
